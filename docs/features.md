# Features

## Authentication

The platform provides secure user authentication and role-based access control. Users can log in using their credentials, and permissions are assigned based on their role (Employee, Reviewer, Manager, or Administrator). This ensures secure access to project resources and protects sensitive decision data.

---

## Decision Management

Users can create, edit, update, and manage decision records throughout their lifecycle. Each decision includes objectives, context, reasoning, supporting documents, and outcomes, making it easy to organize and revisit important decisions.

---

## Alternative Analysis

The platform allows users to record and compare multiple alternatives before selecting the final decision. Each alternative can include its advantages, disadvantages, risks, and evaluation criteria, helping users understand why a particular option was chosen.

---

## Discussion Module

The discussion module enables collaboration among team members by allowing comments, suggestions, and feedback on decision records. Employees, reviewers, and managers can communicate directly within the platform, ensuring transparency throughout the review process.

---

## Approval Workflow

The system follows a structured approval process where decisions move through multiple stages. Employees submit decision records, reviewers evaluate them, managers provide final approval when required, and administrators oversee the overall workflow to ensure compliance and quality.

---

## Knowledge Repository

Approved decision records are stored in a centralized knowledge repository. Users can search, browse, and learn from previous expert decisions, creating a valuable knowledge base that supports future decision-making and organizational learning.

---

## Dashboard

The dashboard provides an overview of platform activities through an intuitive interface. Users can view pending tasks, recent decisions, approval status, performance metrics, notifications, and other relevant information based on their role.

---

## Reports

The platform generates detailed reports on decision activities, user performance, approval statistics, and historical trends. Managers and administrators can use these reports to monitor progress, evaluate performance, and support informed decision-making.

---

## Audit Logs

Audit logs record all significant system activities, including user logins, decision creation, updates, approvals, rejections, and administrative actions. These logs improve transparency, accountability, security, and compliance by maintaining a complete history of platform operations.
