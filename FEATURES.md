# Features

## Authentication
- Secure login for users accessing the platform.
- Role-based access for employees, reviewers, managers, and administrators.

## Decision Management
- Create, view, and manage organizational decisions.
- Store structured information such as title, problem, alternatives, criteria, risks, stakeholders, and outcome.

## Alternative Analysis
- Capture and compare multiple alternatives for each decision.
- Support evaluation criteria and decision rationale.

## Discussion Module
- Add comments and feedback on decision records.
- Enable collaboration across teams.

## Approval Workflow
- Route decisions through approval stages.
- Track approvals and maintain governance workflow visibility.
