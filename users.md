
# Users and Roles

## Employee

The Employee is the primary user of the platform who creates and submits decision workflows for review.

### Responsibilities
- Create new decision replay records
- Document decision-making steps
- Upload supporting files and evidence
- Edit own submissions before approval
- View submission status
- Respond to reviewer feedback
- Access previously submitted decision replays

---

## Reviewer

The Reviewer evaluates submitted decision replays to ensure they are complete, accurate, and meet organizational standards.

### Responsibilities
- Review employee submissions
- Verify decision logic and supporting evidence
- Approve or reject submissions
- Provide comments and feedback
- Request modifications when necessary
- Maintain quality and consistency

---

## Manager

The Manager oversees teams and monitors the overall decision replay process.

### Responsibilities
- Monitor employee performance
- View team decision records
- Track submission progress
- Analyze decision reports and analytics
- Assign reviewers when required
- Approve high-priority decision records
- Generate team performance reports

---

## Administrator

The Administrator has full control over the platform and manages users, system settings, and security.

### Responsibilities
- Manage users and roles
- Create, update, and delete user accounts
- Assign permissions
- Monitor system activities
- Manage decision categories
- Maintain database and system configurations
- Generate organization-wide reports
- Ensure data security and system availability
- Manage backups and platform maintenance

---
