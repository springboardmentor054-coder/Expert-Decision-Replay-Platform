# Users

## Employee
- Creates and updates decision records.
- Adds context such as problem statements, alternatives, risks, and outcomes.
- Participates in discussion threads around decisions.

## Reviewer
- Reviews submitted decisions for completeness and quality.
- Provides feedback and comments for improvement.
- Supports the approval process before escalation.

## Manager
- Oversees important decisions and business impact.
- Approves or rejects decision records.
- Monitors progress and governance status.

## Administrator
- Manages access, platform settings, and system oversight.
- Monitors audit trails, reports, and overall platform usage.
- Ensures governance policies are followed.
