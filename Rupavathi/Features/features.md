# Platform Features

The Expert Decision Replay Platform is designed to help organizations capture, manage, review, and learn from important decisions. It centralizes decision-related information, improves collaboration, and preserves decision history for future reference.

---

## 1. Authentication & Access Control

The platform includes a secure authentication system to ensure that only authorized users can access resources and perform actions based on their roles.

### Key Capabilities
- User registration and login
- Secure password hashing and verification
- JWT-based authentication for protected APIs
- Role-based access control for Employees, Reviewers, Managers, and Administrators
- Session handling and access validation
- Password reset and account verification support

### Benefits
- Protects sensitive organizational data
- Restricts access to only permitted users
- Ensures secure API communication

---

## 2. Decision Management

This module is the core of the platform and allows users to create, organize, update, and monitor decisions throughout their lifecycle.

### Key Capabilities
- Create, edit, and manage decisions
- Organize decisions using categories and tags
- Track decision status from draft to archive
- Attach supporting documents and files
- Maintain version history for updates and changes
- View decision timeline and progress

### Decision Lifecycle
- Draft
- Under Review
- Approved
- Rejected
- Archived

### Benefits
- Keeps all decision records in one place
- Makes decision tracking transparent and structured
- Helps teams revisit past decisions with full context

---

## 3. Alternative Analysis

Before finalizing a decision, users can evaluate multiple alternatives and compare them using different business factors.

### Key Capabilities
- Add and manage multiple alternatives for a decision
- Compare pros and cons of each option
- Perform cost and feasibility analysis
- Identify risks associated with alternatives
- Apply scoring or ranking methods for comparison
- Generate a recommendation summary

### Benefits
- Supports more informed decision-making
- Reduces the chance of biased or incomplete decisions
- Helps teams choose the most practical solution

---

## 4. Discussion & Collaboration

This module allows team members to discuss decisions, provide feedback, and document reasoning before approval.

### Key Capabilities
- Add comments and discussion threads
- Mention team members for clarification or review
- Store meeting notes and observations
- Document decision rationale and supporting explanations
- Upload related files and evidence
- Maintain an activity timeline for discussions

### Benefits
- Encourages collaborative decision-making
- Preserves important discussions and reasoning
- Makes communication around decisions easier to track

---

## 5. Approval Workflow

The platform supports a structured approval mechanism to ensure that decisions are reviewed and validated at the right levels.

### Key Capabilities
- Assign reviewers for decision evaluation
- Support multi-level approval flow
- Track approval history and reviewer actions
- Send notifications for approvals and pending actions
- Request changes before final approval
- Escalate delayed approvals when needed
- Record final approval or rejection status

### Benefits
- Ensures accountability in decision approval
- Reduces confusion in review processes
- Helps maintain a formal decision governance system

---

## 6. Knowledge Repository

The platform acts as a knowledge base where historical decisions are stored and can be searched later for learning and reference.

### Key Capabilities
- Search decisions by title, keywords, or category
- Filter decisions using categories and tags
- Access archived documents and decision history
- View decision timelines and related records
- Track recently viewed decisions
- Support advanced search for faster retrieval

### Benefits
- Preserves organizational knowledge
- Prevents teams from repeating the same mistakes
- Makes previous decisions reusable for future planning

---

## 7. Dashboard

Role-based dashboards provide users with a quick summary of their activities, pending work, and decision statistics.

### Dashboard Views

#### Employee Dashboard
- My decisions
- Pending reviews
- Recent activities
- Decision status overview

#### Reviewer Dashboard
- Assigned decisions for review
- Pending review tasks
- Review history
- Review performance insights

#### Manager Dashboard
- Team decision summary
- Pending approvals
- Decision and approval statistics
- Workflow performance overview

#### Administrator Dashboard
- User and team statistics
- System activity overview
- Audit summaries
- Platform usage insights

### Benefits
- Gives users a quick operational overview
- Improves visibility into pending tasks and decision progress
- Helps managers monitor team performance

---

## 8. Reports & Export

The reporting module helps organizations analyze decisions, approvals, team activity, and overall platform usage.

### Report Types
- Decision reports
- Approval reports
- Team reports
- User activity reports
- Audit reports
- Performance reports

### Export Support
- PDF export
- Excel export
- CSV export

### Benefits
- Simplifies reporting and data sharing
- Supports compliance and management reviews
- Helps analyze trends and performance

---

## 9. Audit Logs & Activity Tracking

The platform maintains detailed logs of important activities for transparency, compliance, and system monitoring.

### Key Capabilities
- Track user activity logs
- Maintain decision change history
- Store version updates and approval logs
- Record login and access history
- Capture security-related events
- Maintain system event logs for monitoring

### Benefits
- Improves accountability and transparency
- Supports auditing and compliance requirements
- Helps identify unauthorized or unusual activities

# Key Highlights

- Secure authentication with JWT
- Role-based access control for different user roles
- Centralized decision recording and tracking
- Alternative comparison with cost, feasibility, and risk analysis
- Collaborative discussion and reasoning capture
- Structured multi-level approval workflow
- Searchable knowledge repository for historical decisions
- Role-based dashboards with useful insights
- Detailed reports and export support

# Conclusion

The Expert Decision Replay Platform is more than a decision recording system. It is a complete organizational decision management solution that supports secure access, structured workflows, collaboration, approval processes, knowledge preservation, and performance reporting. By combining these features, the platform helps organizations make better decisions, maintain transparency, and build a reusable knowledge base for the future.
